Benefits of Cybersmarties for Schools

Cybersmarties is a Proven Safe Educational Social Network Training Platform for Children at primary school. Cybersmarties is a “Safe Haven” for children to be children while at the same time learning key skills in a very practical way so they become better equipped to engage with the cyberworld. Benefits of Cybersmarties for Schools include:

  • Your School will be keeping your Students Safe:
    We Protect Children – It’s our job. Cybersmarties is for children only, meaning that no adult can get access to a child on our platform. According to UNICEF, there are 250,000 predators on the internet at any one time who are using game networks, chat rooms and childrens apps to imitate a child so that they can get access to a child. Since COVID began there is a 400% increase in online predator as children are on the internet more often. This is not possible on Cybersmarties as each child is authenticated as a real child, chats are restricted to the verified children in their class only as approved by teachers.

  • Awarded the Irish Patent for Safety: Cybersmarties is the first social network in the world to be endorsed by a National Police Force as being 100% safe. We were awarded this patent as a result of 2.5 years working with the An Garda Siochána (National Police Force of Ireland) Child Protection Unit. We are very proud to hold this patent.

  • Schools decide the boundaries: When a school signs up with Cybersmarties, they decide how far a student can communicate for example a teacher can decide to allow students to communicate with the kids in their class only, other classes or the whole school. The school is always in control. In general most schools allow the kids in each class to communicate with the other kids in their class only.

  • We want a Different World for Children: Everyone knows that social media and networks have resulted in terrible cyberbullying on a global scale. Cybersmarties uses behavioural technology to educate children as they are sending messages to their friends and identifies if it is negative and wont allow the message to be sent until it is corrected. On a daily basis, it tells children why friends might be upset if they send a negative message and promotes positive online behaviour.

  • Positive Mental Health: There is a growing concern about the rising levels of mental health issues among young children. To combat this, Cybersmarties has a Wellbeing Assistant called Claire (A Cartoon Character) who pops in each day to every child and asks them how their day is going. If they are experiencing problems or anxiety, they will be shown a video on how to deal with that problem so children learn the key skills on how to deal with emotional problems early.

  • Healthy Mind is a Healthy Body: At Cybersmarties we believe that there is a time and a place for everything and it is important that children keep fit and healthy. There are lots of fitness videos for children to follow which are fun and easy to follow at home or in school. We also have nutritional and healthy eating videos for children to watch.

  • UNICEF Global Partner: Cybersmarties is a UNICEF End Violence Against Children Global Partner and work closely with their online safety teams.

  • Online Classes: Teachers can provide homework to their class and each student individually. Students can access the homework through our platform, give the answers and send back to the teachers who can give grades. Teachers can add a timeframe for the homework to be completed and can add videos, links etc within the homework.

  • Coding Curriculum: Each child on Cybersmarties has the ability to learn coding through our partners Code.org from beginner to advanced level.

  • Ease of Use: Cybersmarties can be used at school, at home or on their smartphone or other device.

  • Online Digital Safety Certificate for Teachers and Students: Cybersmarties is the leading internet safety education provider because we have been keeping children safe for five years on a live social network. We have learned a lot over the years and now provide that experience and knowledge to teachers, parents and children. Our Cybersmarties Academy provide an Online Digital Safety Course for Teachers with a Certificate in Cybersafety when the course is completed. In this course you will learn:

    1. Know the Lingo: Online Terms and Their Meaning
    2. Where are the Dangers?: Identifying Potential Risks to Children
    3. Tell Tale Signs of Cyberbullying & How to deal with a Cyberbullying Incident
    4. Filtering Technologies & Where to Report An Issue
    5. Practical Scenarios to Roleplay

Benefits of Cybersmarties for Parents

Most parents did not grow up with technology. However for our children, technology s part of their lives today. We all know there are a lot of positive things about the internet and technology but there are also a lot of dangers. Here are some for the benefits for your children and YOU from using Cybersmarties.

  • Peace of mind:
    Parents can be assured that their children are using the safest social network in the world. Cybersmarties is the only social network that has been endorsed by a National Police Force and is the only social network to be awarded a Patent for Safety. Cybersmarties is proud to also be accepted as a UNICEF Global Partner. Cybersmarties is fully monitored and is for children only. No adult can get communicate with a child on Cybersmarties.

  • Education:
    It is important that children have access to education in the safest way possible. Cybersmarties provides an online homework section which allows teachers to send homework to students, receive the homework back and correct it and give it a grade so your child never loses out on their education.

  • Coding Curriculum:
    Learning to code is very important in todays world. On Cybersmarties, children can learn to code from beginner to advanced level through our partners Code.org.

  • Positive Habits:
    Cybersmarties uses behavioural and emotional technology to build positive habits in children form an early age and also how to cope with problems as they arise. They are taught how to send messages to other people in a positive way, how to protect themselves online and how to report an issue if they are being cyberbullied. Within Cybersmarties each message is fully monitored and if there is an incident of cyberbullying, it is immediately reported by us to the school so it can be dealt with quickly.

  • Online Digital Safety Certificate for Teachers and Students:
    Cybersmarties is the leading internet safety education provider because we have been keeping children safe for five years on a live social network. We have learned a lot over the years and now provide that experience and knowledge to teachers, parents and children. Our Cybersmarties Academy provide an Online Digital Safety Course for Teachers with a Certificate in Cybersafety when the course is completed. In this course you will learn:

    1. Know the Lingo: Online Terms and Their Meaning
    2. Where are the Dangers?: Identifying Potential Risks to your Child
    3. Tell Tale Signs of Cyberbullying
    4. How to deal with a Cyberbullying Incident
    5. Filtering Technologies & Where to Report An Issue
    6. What if my child is the Cyberbully –What can I do

  • Ease of Use:
    Cybersmarties can be used at school, at home or on their smartphone or other device.

Benefits of Cybersmarties for Children

The internet is a wonderful place for children to learn, have fun and interact with each other. It is also a place where it is important to behave in a positive way, to have healthy interactions, to guard over your privacy, to be careful with the information you share with others and understand what is real and unreal on the internet. With Cybersmarties, you learn all this information while also being able to chat with your friends. Here are some benefits for Children:

  • Fun:
    It’s all about FUN!!. Watch cartoons, have a laugh at our jokes and send some in to us, listen to music, paint and draw, read kids news.

  • Games & Cartoons:
    We have hundreds of games and cartoons for you to play and watch. Have you ever thought about designing your own game? First you need to learn how to code. It’s easy and you will love how we do it with our friends from the US Code.org.

  • Chat:
    Send messages to your friends, make new friends and chat just like the grown-ups do.

  • School:
    You can see what is going on in school, check what homework your teacher has sent you, show your parents a teacher’s note, see what your class is up to.

  • About Me:
    Design your own profile, choose an avatar and colour background, list all your interests, let all your friends know how wonderful you are.

  • What Matters:
    We can all feel sad some days. It’s normal, everybody does so its important to think and feel good. Every day we will send you a message telling you how great you are and how you can do anything you want to if you try hard enough. It’s all about feeling happy and staying positive. Our Wellbeing Assistant CLAIRE, pops in every day to say hi and ask you how your day is going. If you are having a bad day, CLAIRE will show you a video about how to deal with that problem.

  • Stay Healthy:
    It is important to stay fit and healthy so you have more energy to play with your friends. We have a fun Stay Healthy Course to follow so you can do exercises at home or at school which are really fun to do.

  • Ease of Use:
    Cybersmarties can be used at school, at home or on their smartphone or other device.

  • Online Digital Safety Certificate for Children:
    Would you like to learn more about how to have fun on the internet but also keep yourself safe? While you learn on Cybersmarties, you can also apply what you have learned by completing our course and get a Certificate to hang on your wall to show parents.

CYBERSMARTIES ACADEMY

Cybersmarties Digital Academy together with UNICEF will provide an Online Digital Safety course for Kids, Teachers & Parents. Schools will be awarded a Digital Safe School upon successful individual certification that comes as a premium feature. Kids can learn English, Science and Mathematics directly from the top lecturers in the world that lay strong academic foundation for growing kids and complements the school curriculum.

CYBERSMARTIES DATAWAREHOUSE

Our Cybersmarties Data Warehouse Team will collate information on children’s educational standards and personal development which can be made available to government departments and schools for media and statistical purposes. This would be “live data” evaluation on potentially millions of students without compromising their data rights as individuals.

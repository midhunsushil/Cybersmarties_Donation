Mental Health & Saftey

As parents, we have learned life lessons. We know that life can sometimes be cruel, unfair, and unreasonable. We like to instill positive life skills in children, which will provide them with the coping mechanisms to deal with life as well as have a positive outlook and be happy. Every day, 7 days a week, each child is sent positive messages and videos to boost self-esteem and confidence. We provide videos on meditation (proven in UK schools to help calm children and help them concentrate), nutrition, physical education, learn how to code to help logic and reasoning skills, and so much more. Of course, we also have Claire Bot, who checks in on each child every day to ask them how their day is going and provides useful videos on how to deal with particular issues such as anxiety. Cybersmarties encourages continuous positivity in a locked-down safe space to allow kids to be kids.

POSITIVE MENTAL HEALTH:

There is a growing concern about the rising levels of mental health issues among young children. To combat this, Cybersmarties has a Wellbeing Assistant called Claire (A Cartoon Character) who pops in each day to every child and asks them how their day is going. If they are experiencing problems or anxiety, they will be shown a video on how to deal with that problem, so children learn the key skills on how to deal with emotional problems early.

HEALTHY MIND IS A HEALTHY BODY:

At Cybersmarties we believe that there is a time and a place for everything and it is important that children keep fit and healthy. There are lots of fitness videos for children to follow which are fun and easy to follow at home or in school. We also have nutritional and healthy eating videos for children to watch.

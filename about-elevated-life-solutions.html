Why Elevated Life Solutions

Fifty (50%) percent of our world is without access to computers and the internet. Yet billions of dollars of technology go wasted every year.​

​This is like feeding and caring for only one of the bulls! ​​​Which causes our bullock cart to move in circles ​and half of the world’s population misses out on the life-altering connectivity to the rest of the world.

How can we truly move forward when only half of us are cared for?

​This is simply no longer acceptable! Our Intent is shifting this! For only when Equally together... We move forward! ​​ Elevated Life Solutions Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

WAYS WE HELP

Our Free Laptop program includes Training, Education and Support provided at no cost to a child or individuals.

Elevated Life Solutions will also train and educate people on how to refurbish older computers, How to use audio/video conferencing software and provide weekly/monthly support, so that they can deploy these computers within their own community and train others to do the same. Thereby furthering the education, standard of living, and increasing the socio-economic viability of the whole community.

Elevated Life Solutions also engages local community leaders to connect and provide additional free education/services/training for their community.

​Your $500 USD donation supports one (1) Laptop and sixty (60) Days of training and support for under privileged / low income communities.

Elevated Life Solutions provides not only free training on how to use the laptop, but also a free Email account, free open source Libre Office Suite (Word processor, Spreadsheet, Presentation, Graphics, PDF Editor & Creator), free Zoom Audio/Video Conferencing client, free Remote Support software, and a Free 5 Gigabyte Cloud (third party) account for data backup/sync.

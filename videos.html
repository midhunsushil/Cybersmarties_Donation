Let's Change the World Together!

Get Green Inc.

TAGLINE:

Improving the World One Reader at a Time

MISSION:

The mission of Get Green is to transform struggling and reluctant readers into more passionate and proficient readers by teaching strategies that make reading fun and turning it into a daily, life-long habit.

WHY:

What good is it teaching students HOW to read if they never WANT to read?

We teach children WHY to read.

As a parent, you probably have never had to tell your child to watch television...
And you’ve probably never had to tell your child to play a video game...
So why should you have to tell your child to read? Reading should be so enjoyable that children choose to do it in their free time because they love it.

The more excited a child is to read, the more likely they are to read. And the more they read, the better they get.

Research shows that the frequency of time spent reading for pleasure is one of the greatest indicators of how well a student performs in reading.

And reading is the key to success in all areas.

We are proud to partner with CyberSmarties because together we believe that building positive habits in young children is essential.

WHAT WE DO:

“We are what we repeatedly do,” said Aristotle. “Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit.”

By building positive daily reading habits in children, we ensure the prosperity of our future.

We bring families together by teaching children how to love reading. No drill and kills. No high-stakes testing. Just fun, meaningful content that makes reading something everyone will love. When you love something, it doesn’t matter what obstacles people throw in your way: you find a way to do it.

It all starts with reading.

In just over two months, we help parents help their children read MORE, read BETTER and LOVE reading!
